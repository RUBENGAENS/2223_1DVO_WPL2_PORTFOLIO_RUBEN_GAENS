Introduction

For Duf, I designed the complete branding and visual identity, aimed at capturing the essence of skate culture—freedom, creativity, and urban expression.
Background
The project involved developing a unique logo and creating original illustrations for the skateboard decks, all infused with a bold and playful style. Each element was crafted to reflect the energy and attitude of the skate community while maintaining a cohesive brand identity. The brand Duf represents more than just skateboarding, it’s a lifestyle. Through a combination of striking visuals, dynamic typography, and expressive graphics, Duf tells the story of a brand that thrives on creativity and movement.

Every design decision, from the color palette to the custom illustrations, is intended to resonate with the skaters’ spirit of individuality and self-expression. The visual identity is energetic and modern, making Duf not only stand out in the skateboarding world but also appeal to a broader streetwear and urban culture audience.
