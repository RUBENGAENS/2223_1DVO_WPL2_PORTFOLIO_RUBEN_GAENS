Ori

Introduction

Ori is an app I developed with four other fellow students on assignment for the company Accenture Song. The app is a digital version of the children's book that contains all the details of your birth.
Background
We were looking for a short and powerful name with a meaning attached. That's how we came up with the name ‘Ori’. Ori is a Hebrew boy's name that means ‘my light’ or ‘new beginning’. In the app, we wanted to offer help to anyone who needs it, during and after pregnancy. We did this through the digitised child's booklet and being in direct contact with the healthcare provider. What I have mainly contributed to within this project is the creation of the digital child booklet, networking opportunities with other parents and healthcare provision. Ori's app offers several useful features, including a shared calendar that can also be used by caregivers. You can also save photos of your child on a clear timeline, making it easy to track their growth.

We designed both the full UI and UX of the project, as well as the accompanying website. In doing so, we ensured a seamless user experience and an attractive, user-friendly design tailored to the needs of the target audience.
