Nimbu

Introduction

During my internship at Zenjoy, I did a rebranding of the CMS system Nimbu. The intention was to modernise the original landing page. This was my final assignment of my graduate thesis for PXL.
Background
Over a period of 3 months, I conducted a full research and survey on the users of the original CMS system. Before I started working on my new landing page, I conducted interviews to better meet the client's needs. The landing page was coded using HTML, CSS, Tailwind and javascript.

I then started creating a new logo, landing page, colour palette, 3D logo, introduction video, promotional gift (growth card).
