Motion 1

I created a 3D render of my logo for my portfolio. This gives a realistic view of the logo with more dynamics. More motion designs can be found below and I am still working on other projects.
Background
The logo was designed in Blender. In my free time, I enjoy creating animated visuals, especially when I can combine it with my other passion, music. I find the creative process of blending visual and auditory elements incredibly fulfilling. Designing animations allows me to bring static ideas to life, giving them motion and rhythm. Music adds another layer of emotion and expression to my work, making each project unique and dynamic. Whether it's syncing animations to the beat of a song or creating visual effects that complement a musical piece, the synergy between these two art forms continuously inspires me to push my creative boundaries.
