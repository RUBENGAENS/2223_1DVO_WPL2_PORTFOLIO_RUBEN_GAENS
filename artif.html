Artif Festival

Introduction

Artif festival concept brings together music, art, and immersive experiences in a vibrant atmosphere, designed to celebrate creativity in all its forms. The goal is to create a multi-sensory experience where attendees are inspired, engaged, and connected through live performances, installations, and interactive art.
Background
This festival is not just an event, it's an experience designed to transport attendees into a world of creativity and sensory engagement. The festival aims to merge live music, visual arts, and interactive installations, creating a one-of-a-kind gathering where the boundaries between artist and audience, performer and observer, are blurred. The ultimate goal is to cultivate a space where attendees feel fully immersed, free to explore and connect with the art around them and with each other.

Everything you see below has been created entirely by me, from the logo and brand identity to the promo video and many more.
