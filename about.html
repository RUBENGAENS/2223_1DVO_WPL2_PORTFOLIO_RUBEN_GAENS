My passion for graphic design has grown from an early age. I originally started creating designs for clothing graphics.
Background
I graduated from the 'Digital Design' program at PXL University College in Hasselt. During this program, I learned all the ins and outs of the field. In the final year, I also completed a 7-month internship at Zenjoy in Leuven, where I contributed to various projects. This experience allowed me to apply my theoretical knowledge in a practical setting and work in a professional environment. I learned a lot about meeting deadlines, collaborating with a team of creative professionals, and adapting my designs to the specific needs of clients. This internship period further honed my skills and prepared me well for a career in graphic and motion design.
In graphic design, I create strong visual identities like logos and brochures, always considering the client's brand and message. In motion design, I combine visuals, movement, and sound to create engaging and effective animations. I stay updated with industry trends to ensure each project is successful. In the future, I am eager to learn much more about motion design.
