DP-trappen

Introduction

For DP-Trappen, I handled both the design and development of the website from start to finish.
Background
DP-Trappen is a subsidiary of Eddy De Prins, specializing in custom-made staircases. The website is tailored primarily for professional clients, such as contractors and interior designers, who require a fast and efficient way to place orders.

of the key features of the site is the intuitive product configurator. This tool allows users to customize staircase components to their specific needs and preferences, streamlining the ordering process and reducing the need for back-and-forth communication. The design prioritizes clarity, usability, and performance, ensuring a smooth experience across all devices.

The result is a modern, professional platform that reflects the quality and craftsmanship of the DP-Trappen brand while simplifying the way professionals do business with them.
