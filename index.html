Myself

My name is Ruben Gaens. I am 24 years old and live in Alken. I have a good relationship with others and am resilient, flexible and multifunctional. Furthermore, I am rather assertive, with a positive attitude and a lot of motivation, so I do not shy away from a challenge.

I need variety and room for creativity in my work in order to be goal-oriented and passionate. I am driven to tackle and finish something.
