Introduction

I also enjoy working on print materials for clients, as it combines creativity with technical precision.
Background
During my time at boostU and my internship at Zenjoy, I gained hands-on experience in creating print-ready designs that meet professional production standards. I learned how to properly set up bleeds, margins, and print marks, and how to export files in the correct formats for various types of printing, from flyers and brochures to posters and packaging.

This experience taught me to pay close attention to detail, work efficiently within the constraints of different print processes, and understand the importance of consistency across different formats and materials. I find the tactile aspect of print rewarding — seeing a design come to life on paper adds an extra layer of satisfaction to the creative process.
